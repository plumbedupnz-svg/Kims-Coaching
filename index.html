About Kim

Warm, structured coaching for every stage of junior development.

Based at Maraetai Tennis Club, Kim works with junior players of all ages and abilities - from complete beginners through to competitive interclub and tournament players.

With a strong background in sport, science, and nutrition, Kim takes a holistic approach to coaching that focuses not only on technical development, but also confidence, enjoyment, resilience, and long-term participation in sport.

Coaching Philosophy

The best results come when players feel ready to grow.

Every session is designed to be engaging, structured, and encouraging while building strong technical and tactical foundations.
